A station located on the banks of the Pasig River that serves as a transportation hub for commuters going to and from PUP and other parts of Metro Manila.

A monument located in front of the PUP Main Building. The obelisk is dedicated to the memory of Apolinario Mabini, while the monument itself commemorates the contribution of national heroes and martyrs to Philippine history.
